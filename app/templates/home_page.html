About Us


Need someone to help with move your couch? Need a Pirate Hat for Princetoween? Have a vacuum cleaner lying around? Free and down to fill surveys? No worries - SNBS is HERE for YOU!


Through our research and personal experiences we know that students have everyday needs, other students can fulfill these needs, and we can be the link between these students. We are committed to facilitating business transactions between these students so as to improve the quality of life of both parties involved.

Our History


Our company started in spring of 2018 from a class project at Princeton University. We saw the need to streamline the process of connecting students with a need to students with a solution. Though we had polled interested from many campuses, we implemented our prototype on Princeton’s campus. Since then, we have successfully expanded to campuses all over the east coast and hope to grow nationwide.
